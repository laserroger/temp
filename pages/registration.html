653. WE-Heraeus-Seminar

Optical Microcavities and Their Applications

Registration

Application

Due to the generous support of the WE-Heraeus Foundation, there will be no conference fee, but all participants are expected to contribute to the scientific program of the workshop and to stay for the whole length of the program. Meals and accommodation will be covered by the WE-Heraeus Foundation, but the number of participants is limited. The organizers will therefore make a selection of applications.

In addition to the invited talks, there will be posters for which applicants can apply. Participants will receive a notification of whether their poster has been selected together with their acceptance.

To apply, please send an e-mail with your full name, institution, position, and the final version of your poster here. Further revision is acceptable by directly upload again.

Invited speakers do not need to apply but will be contacted by the WE-Heraeus Foundation separately.

  • Application deadline: 31. August 2017
  • Notification of acceptance: Begining/middle of September
